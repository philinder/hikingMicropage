About the Project

Philipp is quite bored and would like to invite you, your boy-/girlfriend and/or family to join this small summer sport project.

Sunday from now on is hiking day. Each sunday during the summer holidays there will be a tour through the austrian mountains.

You and your folks can join whenever you have time and want to. - just add your name in the doodle list.

Depending on the people registered for the hike, we can adjust the tour and the activity. We will start relatively early each sunday - so that you will have some time for other activities.
